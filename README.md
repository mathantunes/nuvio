# 🌍 Globudget

Globudget is a simple, free, and self-hosted application for tracking assets, budgeting, and expenses across multiple currencies.

## Why Globudget?

As an immigrant, managing finances across countries can get messy. Income and daily expenses may be tied to one country, while savings, investments, or obligations exist elsewhere.

Globudget was built to solve that problem:

* Keep track of assets in different countries
* Work with real exchange rates
* Plan transfers without breaking your budget

## Features

* 💱 Multi-currency support with real exchange rate tracking
* 💼 Asset tracking across different countries
* 📊 Budget planning and expense tracking
* 🔄 Clear visibility when moving money between currencies

## Use Case

Globudget helps you answer questions like:

* *Can I afford to send money abroad this month?*
* *How do currency changes impact my total net worth?*
* *Am I still within budget after an international transfer?*

## Philosophy

This project is intentionally:

* **Simple** – focused on core financial clarity
* **Transparent** – no hidden calculations
* **Self-hosted** – your data stays yours
